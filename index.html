Meet Our Medical Team

Our experienced healthcare professionals are dedicated to providing exceptional medical care with compassion and expertise.

Dr. Randall Mercier M.D.

Internal Medicine Physician

Dr. Mercier earned his medical degree from Tulane University School of Medicine and completed his internship and residency training at Duke University Medical Center. Board certified by the American Board of Internal Medicine and a Fellow of the American College of Physicians, he brings over four decades of distinguished experience in internal medicine with comprehensive expertise in proactive wellness and preventive healthcare.

Board Certified Internal Medicine Fellow, American College of Physicians MD, Tulane University 40+ Years Experience

Sarah Johnson, RN

Registered Nurse

Sarah has been providing compassionate nursing care for over 10 years. She specializes in patient education, chronic care management, and ensuring each patient receives personalized attention during their visit.

BSN from UNC Chapel Hill Certified in Chronic Care

Michael Chen, PA-C

Physician Assistant

Michael works closely with Dr. Mercier to provide comprehensive care. With expertise in preventive medicine and acute care, he ensures continuity of care and maintains the highest standards of patient service.

Master's in Physician Assistant Studies NCCPA Certified
